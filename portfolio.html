Project 1 (Lyricube)

The project 1 concept is called the lyricube. The concept of the lyricube comes from wanting to solve situations when there are song we might want to listen to or have heard from somewhere but don't really remeber it except from a line or two from the song, although apps like shazam helps to identify songs, that can only be done if the excert song was being played to the shazam. The lyricube allows the users for search out and find a song they want to remember by simply using a word or paragraph of what the users remember from the song and when they search using the word or the paragraph, the application brings out songs that are most closely related to the lyrics, we also included a feature for the application to also search out the videos of the songs found on youtube and attach the videos with the result as one.

Daily planner

The daily planner is an app for people to log in things to do through out the day from 9am to 5pm, when the user logs in a to-do in a time block of the daily planner, the log is saved in that time block also the time blocks are responsive to the current time of the day with the block
eing red when the time has gone past, green when the time is to come and grey when it is the current time.
